Overview
What is ACCES-VR?
ACCES-VR (Adult Career and Continuing Education Services – Vocational Rehabilitation) is a program of the NY State Education Department that supports individuals with disabilities in preparing for and obtaining employment — including funding for barber school.
What ACCES-VR May Cover
✂
Tuition and program fees (most approved cases)
✂
Books and educational materials
✂
Barbering tools and supplies (case-dependent)
✂
Transportation assistance (case-dependent)
✂
Support services during training
Step by Step
How to Use ACCES-VR at ABI
01
Check Eligibility
ACCES-VR serves individuals with disabilities that create a barrier to employment. Contact your local office to see if you qualify.
02
Apply to ACCES-VR
You'll be assigned a Vocational Rehabilitation Counselor who builds your Individualized Plan for Employment (IPE).
03
List ABI as Your School
We provide all documentation your counselor needs.
04
ABI Coordinates
Once approved, ABI coordinates directly with ACCES-VR for tuition and covered costs.
05
Start Training
Begin your path to a Master Barber license — with financial barriers removed.

FAQ
Common Questions
01What disabilities qualify?
∧
ACCES-VR serves a wide range of physical, mental health, learning, sensory, and other disabilities. Eligibility is assessed case-by-case by your VR counselor.
02Is there a cost to apply?
∨
No. Applying to ACCES-VR and working with a counselor is free.
03How long does approval take?
∨
Typically several weeks after your initial application — start as early as possible.
04Can ABI help with the process?
∨
Yes. Our admissions team is experienced with ACCES-VR referrals and provides all necessary documentation.
