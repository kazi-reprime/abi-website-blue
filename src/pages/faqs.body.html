Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs
Barber School Questions, Answered
01How long does the program take?
∧
The 500 Hour Master Barber Program can be completed in as little as 4 months depending on schedule. The 50 Hour Refresher is a 2-week intensive. The 3 Hour Contagious Diseases program is a single session.
02When do new classes start?
∨
New classes begin the first Monday of every month.
03What schedules are available?
∨
Plan A Morning (Mon–Fri 8AM–2PM), Plan B Afternoon (Mon–Fri 2PM–8PM), Plan C Weekend (Sat–Sun 9AM–7PM).
04What are the entrance requirements?
∨
SSN card; HS Diploma/GED or ATB exam at ABI; at least 17; proof of address; photo ID; $500 down payment.
05How much does the program cost?
∨
The 500 Hour program starts at $4,600 (afternoon/weekend) or $5,600 (morning). The 50 Hour Refresher is $1,500. Payment plans available.
06Do you offer payment plans?
∨
Yes — flexible weekly plans after your down payment. Plan B: $500 down + 16×$250 + final $100.
07What license will I receive?
∨
You'll be prepared for the NY State Board of Barbering Exam and your Master Barber License.
08Do you offer job placement?
∨
Yes — our Job Placement Office helps graduates connect with employers and explore opportunities.
09Where are your locations?
∨
Manhattan: 48 West 39th Street, NY 10018. Bronx: 121 Westchester Square, NY 10461.
10Do you accept GI Bill® or VA benefits?
∨
Yes — Post-9/11 GI Bill® and VA education benefits are accepted.
11What is ACCES-VR?
∨
A NY State program that may fund vocational training for eligible individuals with disabilities.
12Do I need to buy my own tools?
∨
Yes — tools, books and supplies are purchased separately, from ABI or other suppliers.
13Is ABI licensed by the state?
∨
Yes — licensed by the New York State Department of Education, 30+ years in business.
14Can I get a haircut at ABI?
∨
Yes — affordable student haircuts (around $3). See the Haircuts page.
