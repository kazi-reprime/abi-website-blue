Start with the Right Training

To become a barber in New York, you must complete required training and meet state licensing criteria.

At American Barber Institute, we offer a 500-hour Master Barber Training Program designed to prepare you for:

  • Real-world barbering skills
  • Client interaction and professionalism
  • State licensing requirements
  • Career readiness

This program focuses on both fundamentals and modern techniques, so you're ready for today's industry.

Hands-On Experience That Matters

Barbering is a hands-on profession — you learn best by doing.

That's why our program emphasizes:

  • Training with real clients
  • Repetition of core techniques
  • Building confidence behind the chair

You don't just learn theory — you develop real skill.

Health & Safety Training

New York State requires barbers to complete a 3-hour HIV/AIDS & Contagious Diseases course.

This ensures you understand:

  • Sanitation practices
  • Health and safety standards
  • Client protection

Professionalism includes maintaining a safe and clean environment at all times.

We're here to help you succeed from day one through graduation.
