## FAQ answers (richer, verbatim facts)
- Cost: 500-hr starts $4,600 (afternoon/weekend) or $5,600 (morning); $500–$550 down + weekly $150–$300; books/tools extra; ACCES-VR, GI Bill®, VA accepted.
- Length: NY requires 500 hrs; full-time ~4 months (17 wks @30hr); weekend ~6–7 months (27 wks).
- Hours/week: FT 30 hrs (AM 8–2 or PM 2–8); weekend 18 hrs Sat–Sun.
- Diploma: HS diploma/GED or pass ATB exam at ABI; must be 17+.
- Online? No — NY requires in-person hands-on; practice on real clients from first weeks.
- License: prepares for NY State Master Barber license + Board Exam prep; job placement office.
- Financial aid: ACCES-VR (covers tuition/tools/books for qualified NYers w/ disabilities), Post-9/11 GI Bill®, VA, NYS Dept of Labor grants, weekly payments.
- Start dates: first Monday of every month, both campuses.

## PROGRAMS (full list — we were missing most)
1. 500 Hour Master Barber — 4 months · 30 hrs/wk · AM/PM/Weekend — from $4,600, plans available. Flagship, real clients, State Board prep, job placement.
2. 200 Hour Barber Fundamentals — 2 months · M–F 2:00–7:00 PM — $3,600 ($1,200 down + weekly). For apprentices, licensed cosmetologists, operators.
3. 50 Hour Barber Refresher — 2 weeks · M–F AM or PM — $1,500 (two payments). For cosmetologists, hairdressers, apprentices; pass NY Board.
4. Scalp Micro-Pigmentation (SMP) — 4 weekends · Sat&Sun 11–5 — $3,500 (tool kit included). Hairline design, pigments, device technique, client care.
5. 3 Hour Contagious Diseases — Home study by mail — $100 (one payment). Required for every new NY barber application + renewal; two certificates by mail.
6. License Transfer — out-of-state & international — free qualification check. ABI manages full NYS licensure/reciprocity application.
