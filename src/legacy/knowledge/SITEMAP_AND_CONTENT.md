## Programs
1. **500 Hour Master Barber** — flagship, ~4 months, 500 hrs, from $4,600. NY State Board prep.
2. **50 Hour Barber Refresher** — 2 weeks, 50 hrs, $1,500, certificate, for licensed pros.
3. **3 Hour Contagious Diseases** — 1 session, HIV/bloodborne pathogen course required for NY board. Pricing on request.
4. **500 Hour Master Barber — Bronx** (540-hour-master-barber-bronx route).

- Plan A Full-Time Morning: Mon–Fri 8AM–2PM — $5,600 — $500 down + 17×$300 (Most Popular on mobile)

## Entrance requirements
SSN card · HS Diploma/GED or pass ATB exam at ABI · 17+ · proof of address · photo ID/license · $500 down ($100 reg fee).
