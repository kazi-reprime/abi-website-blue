Modern-Day Barbering Problems — and How to Overcome Them

From staff shortages to keeping up with trends, today's barbershop owners face real challenges. Here are practical fixes that protect your craft and your business.

December 10, 2023

Barbers play a powerful role in helping people look and feel their best. While barbering is a rewarding career, it comes with real challenges — especially in today's fast-moving industry.

From hiring staff to keeping up with trends, barbershop owners and barbers must constantly adapt.

👉 Here's how to overcome the most common modern-day barbering challenges.

Staff Shortages

Finding the right barbers can be difficult — but the right approach makes all the difference.

✅ How to fix it:

  • Post detailed job listings on platforms like LinkedIn and Indeed
  • Offer competitive pay or booth rental options
  • Expand your search beyond your immediate area
  • Use recruitment services if needed — but always make the final decision yourself

The goal is quality, not just filling a chair.

Staff Training & Development

Even experienced barbers need guidance when joining your shop.

✅ How to fix it:

  • Hire candidates with barber or cosmetology school experience
  • Train them in your shop's standards and techniques
  • Keep them updated on modern styles and trends
  • Evaluate performance after training

Training is an investment — not a cost.

Finding the Right Products

The products you carry directly affect your brand and revenue.

✅ How to fix it:

  • Track what your clients actually use and request
  • Buy wholesale to increase profit margins
  • Build relationships with product suppliers
  • Stay updated on new and trending grooming products

Sell what your clients trust — not just what's popular.

Building Customer Loyalty

Loyal clients are the foundation of a successful barbershop.

✅ How to fix it:

  • Collect client contact information
  • Send appointment reminders and promotions
  • Stay active on social media
  • Create a consistent, reliable experience

Loyalty = repeat business + referrals.

Creating a Welcoming Experience

First impressions matter — and so does consistency.

✅ How to fix it:

  • Greet every client professionally
  • Learn and remember names
  • Maintain a clean, comfortable shop
  • Provide small touches:
    • Charging outlets
    • Entertainment
    • Comfortable waiting area

People come back for how you make them feel.

Keeping Up With Trends

The barbering industry evolves quickly — styles change constantly.

✅ How to fix it:

  • Follow industry leaders and barbers online
  • Stay active on platforms like Instagram and TikTok
  • Experiment with new styles, techniques, and tools
  • Talk to clients about what they're seeing and wanting

Staying current keeps you competitive.

Barber School vs. Real-World Experience

Education gives you the foundation — but growth continues after school.

✅ Key takeaway:

  • Barber school teaches core skills and fundamentals
  • Real-world experience builds speed, confidence, and client relationships

The best barbers combine both.

Final Thoughts

Modern barbering comes with challenges — but every challenge has a solution.

If you:

  • Build the right team
  • Stay consistent
  • Focus on clients
  • Keep learning

You can create a strong, profitable, and lasting business.
