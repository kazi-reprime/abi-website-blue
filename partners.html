Our Partners

For nearly 30 years, ABI graduates have walked out of our classrooms and into careers that build real lives — some open their own shops, some become co-owners, and some join the most respected teams in New York.

Real Shops · Real Careers
One School Behind Every Chair
01
THE 30-YEAR EMPIRE
Levels Barbershop

Founded in 1996 on 125th Street in Harlem by Kamal Nuru, Levels now spans Manhattan, Brooklyn and Orange County — alongside the Antique Barber product line, Barber World TV and the annual NYC Barber Battle. Several Nuru-family co-owners are ABI graduates who earned their licenses with us ~15 years ago, and many licensed barbers at Levels today are ABI alumni. Barber World TV even featured ABI Lead Instructor Harold “Barkim” Brown on Episode 007.

levelsbarbershop.com →
02
ONE BLOCK FROM HOME
Diamond Fadez

One block from ABI's flagship campus at 308 West 39th Street — a combined barbershop and tattoo studio customers call “the best barbershop in the city.” Owned by Mike, a proud ABI graduate and older brother of current ABI bilingual instructor Richard Cancel: same school, same craft, two generations of one family training the next wave.

diamondfadezbarbershop.com →
03
THE TWO-YEAR STORY
Untouchable Cutz

Jimmy was advanced from day one. Just two years after earning his ABI license he opened his own shop; ~15 years later Untouchable Cutz is one of Staten Island's most respected barbershops — a modern space with a team carrying 50+ combined years of experience. Jimmy has hired many ABI graduates and now offers private tutoring and weekly grooming classes.

untouchablecutz.com →
04
THE TRUSTED PARTNER
Expo Gentlemen Salon

Some of the most important stories are about owners who hire our graduates. Joe — the entrepreneur behind Expo Gentlemen Salon, with locations in Staten Island and Brooklyn — has built two thriving shops by trusting ABI to send him the talent he needs, year after year. When Joe needs to hire, he calls ABI first.

Staten Island: 326 New Dorp Lane · (718) 980-1100
05
PRESS-FEATURED PARTNER
Otis & Finn

One of NYC's most celebrated state-of-the-art barbershops — featured in GQ, BuzzFeed, HuffPost, FOX5 and PIX11 — with four locations across Long Island City, Court Square, Greenpoint and Williamsburg. ABI graduate James Cavanaugh has cut professionally for five years since earning his license, and Otis & Finn proudly names ABI on his official bio.

otisandfinn.com →
06
THE LIVING MUSEUM
NYC Barber Shop Museum

On Manhattan's Upper West Side (Columbus Ave & E 70th St, plus Sunny Isles, Miami) — the first museum of its kind: a working barbershop where every cut is performed surrounded by 1,000+ artifacts. Run by Arthur Rubinoff, a fourth-generation barber; opened June 2018, won NY Style Magazine's Best Barbershop (2015), now a 501(c)(3). ABI has placed graduates here for years.

barbershopmuseum.com →
