Jobs & Job Placement

Two ways ABI serves the barbering community: a full-time placement office for our barbers, and free shop registration for owners who want to hire trained, board-prepared graduates.

For Barbers
Job Placement Office
Every ABI student gets a one-on-one meeting with our placement office — a review of the employment prospects waiting for them, tailored to individual goals and skills. We maintain a full-time office for graduates and for students who want to work part-time while still in school.
✓
Four Months Training
Graduate in as little as 4 months — a career-oriented, essentially recession-proof skill.
✓
High Income Potential
Many barbers are their own boss and get paid every day. You decide how hard you work.
✓
Be Your Own Boss
Few careers offer this freedom: control your own income and schedule.
✓
Strong Job Availability
Demand for licensed barbers is strong — many shops need help right now.

We can't guarantee placement — practical skill, conduct and professionalism all matter. During training we prepare you for the real needs of an employer: interviewing, etiquette and the boundaries between you, your employer and clientele.
